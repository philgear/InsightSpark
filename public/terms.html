1. Purpose and Intended Audience

Pivot & Pulse is a cognitive and creative co-thinking application built for two specialized use cases:

  • Creative Mode: Designed for innovators, builders, writers, and problem-solvers to break creative blocks using guided brainstorming prompts (such as Combinatorial Evolution and Chaos Theory).
  • Care Mode: Designed for caregivers, health advocates, family members, and individuals looking to translate complex, de-identified health goals into compassionate, person-centric insights and actionable care plans.

2. Bring Your Own Key (BYOK) Model

The Application runs as a decentralized, client-hosted utility. You must supply your own Google Gemini API Key from Google AI Studio (obtainable at ai.dev) to access the generation capabilities. Your key is stored securely on your browser’s local storage and is sent directly to secure endpoints to fulfill your request. We do not store or log your key on our servers.

You must **never** input Protected Health Information (PHI) or Personally Identifiable Information (PII)—including names, physical addresses, contact numbers, or identifying medical details. Built-in PII validation filters are active, but you hold complete responsibility for the absolute anonymity of your inputs.

4. Client-Side Data Sovereignty

All bookmarks, saved care plans, and history are saved solely to your browser's local cache (localStorage). Because we operate without accounts or databases, we cannot restore or recover your data if you clear your browser cache, reinstall your OS, or change devices.
